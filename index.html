From a young age, I have loved taking things apart to see how they work. In adulthood, this became a great ability to troubleshoot and solve a variety of problems.

I pivoted from fitness and retail management into the world of tech in 2018. I have utilized my unique set of skills to work across Customer Success, Product Management, Technical Support, and Web Development teams. I am seamlessly agile and endlessly curious.

I find myself happiest at the intersection of troubleshooting and client happiness, and enjoy delivering elegant solutions to complex problems.
